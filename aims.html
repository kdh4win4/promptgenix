Specific Aims (Phase I): PromptGenix – BioAI Automation Platform

PromptGenix LLC (Women & Minority-Owned) · Contact: dohoon.kim1@icloud.com · Website: promptgenix.org/sbir.html

Significance

Immunology and infectious disease programs generate large volumes of RNA-Seq, single-cell RNA-Seq, and high-dimensional flow cytometry data, yet analysis often depends on fragmented tools, custom scripts, and scarce expert time. This drives long turnaround, inconsistent reproducibility, and delayed biological interpretation.

The bottleneck is not only computation, but scientific synthesis: translating multi-omic outputs into actionable mechanisms, experiment plans, and study decisions.

Innovation

PromptGenix introduces an integrated BioAI co-investigator that combines end-to-end automated omics pipelines with an internal, literature-aware LLM layer and a Hypothesis Generator. Instead of producing only static plots and summaries, PromptGenix ranks mechanistic hypotheses and proposes concrete follow-up experiments grounded in traceable PubMed-aware reasoning.

Overall objective & central hypothesis

Objective: Deliver a validated, reproducible Phase I prototype that automates RNA-Seq and flow cytometry workflows and generates reviewer-ready reports with ranked hypotheses and suggested validation experiments.

Central hypothesis: Standardized automated pipelines combined with traceable, literature-aware LLM reasoning will reduce analysis turnaround time and increase interpretability, enabling faster, higher-quality study decisions in immunology and infectious disease research.

Specific aims

  • Aim 1. Harden end-to-end automated RNA-Seq and flow cytometry pipelines using public datasets (GEO, SRA, FlowRepository).
  • Aim 2. Implement a traceable Hypothesis Generator that ranks biological hypotheses and proposes validation experiments with PubMed-aware citations.
  • Aim 3. Generate exportable HTML/PDF reports and evaluate usability through 2–3 pilot studies using public or de-identified datasets.

Expected outcomes & deliverables

  • Validated prototype producing reproducible outputs across reruns (versioned configs + reports).
  • Reviewer-ready reports generated within <24 hours from raw data inputs (dataset-dependent).
  • Measured reductions in turnaround time and positive user feedback on AI-generated hypotheses.

Impact

Successful completion of Phase I will establish PromptGenix as a scalable BioAI co-investigator platform for immunology studies. Phase I validation will rely exclusively on public datasets to avoid IP/data-use conflicts, positioning the platform for Phase II expansion into multi-site studies and secure deployments.
