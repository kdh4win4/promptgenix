Company / Team (Phase I)

PromptGenix LLC (Women & Minority-Owned) · Contact: dohoon.kim1@icloud.com · promptgenix.org
Company structure

Women-led operations with founder-led technical execution.

PromptGenix is structured to qualify for women-owned and minority-owned small business programs while maintaining PI-level scientific and engineering execution for SBIR Phase I deliverables. The CEO focuses on operations, compliance, and commercialization readiness, while the founder CTO leads product architecture, pipeline hardening, and validation.

Core differentiation

  • Domain depth: immunology, infectious disease, vaccine/HIV study workflows
  • Technical depth: RNA-Seq pipelines, flow cytometry analytics, reproducible reporting
  • BioAI layer: hypothesis generation grounded in observed signals with traceable citations
  • Deployment realism: local-first execution + optional secure cloud path (AWS VPC)
Automation Reproducibility Interpretability Actionability

Phase I execution capacity

  • Lean team: focused decision-making and rapid iteration
  • Public-data validation: Phase I does not depend on restricted datasets
  • Reviewer-friendly outputs: HTML/PDF reports and traceable reasoning
  • Pilot readiness: structured onboarding + feedback templates for 2–3 pilot labs
Phase I posture: deliver measurable KPIs (turnaround time, reproducibility, usefulness of hypotheses) using public datasets to minimize IP/data-use concerns.
