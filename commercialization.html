Commercialization Plan (Phase I → Phase II)

PromptGenix LLC · Contact: dohoon.kim1@icloud.com · promptgenix.org
Positioning

BioAI “co-investigator” that reduces analysis time and increases scientific actionability.

PromptGenix commercializes an end-to-end automation platform for RNA-Seq and flow cytometry that generates reproducible pipeline outputs and reviewer-friendly interpretation—including ranked hypotheses and concrete follow-up experiments with traceable literature-aware citations.

Primary customers

  • NIH-funded academic labs doing immunology, infectious disease, HIV/vaccine studies
  • Government research groups needing fast, reproducible analytics (secure/on-prem options)
  • CROs & biotech teams seeking scalable turnaround and standardized reporting
  • Core facilities supporting multiple PIs with consistent pipelines and templates
Academic Government CRO / Biotech Core facilities

Value proposition

  • Speed: reduce analysis turnaround from weeks to days (or <24h for standard runs)
  • Reproducibility: versioned configs, deterministic reruns, exportable artifacts
  • Interpretability: plain-language summaries + figures + hypothesis list
  • Actionability: experiment suggestions (flow panels, contrasts, subset analyses)
  • Deployment options: local Mac Studio or secure AWS VPC (data-sensitive workflows)

Business model

Flexible pricing aligned to lab budgets and security requirements.

Initial go-to-market

  • Pilot program: 2–3 partner labs (Phase I) with structured KPIs + usability feedback
  • Letters of support: pilot partners and collaborating PIs (Phase II readiness)
  • Visibility: SBIR portal pages + demo reports on public datasets
Near-term traction: pilots → references → Phase II scale-out.

Revenue options

  • Subscription: per lab / per seat (software + templates + updates)
  • Compute add-on: managed cloud runs (optional) for heavier datasets
  • Service + software: onboarding + customization + reporting packages
  • Enterprise: secure deployment in customer AWS VPC / on-prem environments
SaaS Hybrid On-prem Services
Milestones

Phase I creates a sellable prototype; Phase II scales adoption.

Stage Deliverables Commercial outcome
Phase I Harden pipelines on public datasets; Hypothesis Generator with traceability; reviewer-ready reports; pilot usability and KPI evaluation. Demonstrable value for partner labs; basis for pricing; Phase II-ready product roadmap.
Phase II Expanded dataset coverage; improved UI/automation; multi-site pilots; packaging for secure deployments; support and onboarding workflows. Paid subscriptions and enterprise deployments; repeatable onboarding; scalable revenue.

Phase I establishes technical feasibility and reviewer trust; Phase II will scale PromptGenix to multi-site cohorts, secure deployments, and revenue-generating pilot partnerships with NIH-funded labs and CROs.

Commercial thesis: Labs will adopt PromptGenix when it demonstrably reduces analysis time, improves reproducibility across reruns, and produces hypotheses that directly inform study design decisions.
