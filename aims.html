Specific Aims (Phase I): PromptGenix – BioAI Automation Platform
PromptGenix LLC (Women & Minority-Owned) · Contact: dohoon.kim1@icloud.com · Website: promptgenix.org/sbir.html
Significance

Infectious disease and immunology programs generate large volumes of RNA-Seq, scRNA-Seq, and high-dimensional flow cytometry data, yet analysis remains fragmented across tools, custom scripts, and limited expert time. This slows turnaround, reduces reproducibility, and delays actionable biological interpretation and experiment planning.

Innovation

PromptGenix integrates end-to-end automated omics pipelines with an internal, literature-aware LLM layer and a traceable Hypothesis Generator that ranks mechanistic hypotheses and proposes concrete validation experiments. Unlike generic summarizers, PromptGenix links every claim to observed pipeline outputs and/or PubMed-style citations with uncertainty labels.

Overall objective & central hypothesis

Objective: Deliver a validated, reproducible Phase I prototype that automates RNA-Seq and flow cytometry workflows and generates reviewer-ready reports with ranked hypotheses and suggested validation experiments.

Central hypothesis: Standardized automated pipelines plus traceable, literature-aware LLM reasoning will reduce analysis turnaround time and increase interpretability, enabling faster, higher-quality study design decisions in infectious disease and vaccine research.

Specific aims
  • Aim 1: Harden end-to-end automated RNA-Seq and flow cytometry pipelines using public datasets (GEO/SRA/FlowRepository), with deterministic configs and versioned outputs.
  • Aim 2: Implement a traceable Hypothesis Generator that ranks hypotheses and proposes validation experiments with evidence-linked, PubMed-aware citations and confidence flags.
  • Aim 3: Produce exportable HTML/PDF reports (figures, tables, narrative, hypotheses, experiment plan) and evaluate usability/KPIs with 2–3 pilot-style public-data studies.
Expected outcomes & deliverables
  • Validated prototype producing reproducible outputs across reruns (versioned configs + reports).
  • Reviewer-ready reports generated within <24 hours from raw inputs (dataset-dependent).
  • Measured improvements in turnaround time and user-rated usefulness of AI-generated hypotheses.
Impact

Phase I establishes technical feasibility and reviewer trust; Phase II will scale PromptGenix to multi-site cohorts and secure deployments, expand dataset coverage and UI/automation, and convert validated pilots into revenue-generating subscriptions and enterprise engagements.
