PromptGenix SBIR Portal

PromptGenix is a BioAI hypothesis intelligence engine that integrates public biomedical data and scientific literature to generate ranked, testable hypotheses with quantified uncertainty. This portal provides reviewer-ready Phase I materials aligned to NIH SBIR expectations.

Start: SBIR Overview Specific Aims Research Strategy
Phase I focus

Demonstrate feasibility of an automated, evidence-linked decision engine that prioritizes biological hypotheses using public datasets (e.g., GEO, SRA, FlowRepository) and literature-derived priors.
