Company & Team

PromptGenix is a women- and minority-owned BioAI startup building an automation platform for RNA-Seq and flow cytometry analysis, interpretation, and hypothesis generation. The team combines PI-level immunology/bioinformatics expertise with practical engineering for reproducible pipelines and reviewer-ready reporting.

Contact: dohoon.kim1@icloud.com · Website: https://kdh4win4.github.io
Company structure

Women-owned leadership with founder-led technical execution.

PromptGenix is structured to qualify for women-owned and minority-owned small business programs. The CEO oversees operations, compliance, and commercialization readiness, while the founder CTO leads product architecture and technical delivery for Phase I.

Phase I posture: deliver measurable technical validation using public datasets only (no IP/PHI dependency).
Why we can execute

Deep domain + proven pipelines + practical deployment.

  • Domain depth: immunology, infectious disease, vaccine studies, high-dimensional flow
  • Technical depth: RNA-Seq pipelines, statistics, reporting, automation, AI integration
  • Deployment realism: local-first execution with optional AWS VPC path
  • Reviewer trust: traceable interpretation and PubMed-linked hypothesis generation
