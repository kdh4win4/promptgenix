The Vision

Evidence-Weighted Hypothesis Intelligence

PromptGenix is an AI-driven hypothesis intelligence engine designed to help biomedical researchers navigate vast amounts of public data by converting raw evidence into ranked, traceable, and statistically-grounded scientific insights.

The Problem

Biomedical data is exploding, but human synthesis capacity remains stagnant. Current AI tools often rely on generative LLMs that suffer from stochastic hallucinations and a lack of formal uncertainty quantification. This leads to a reproducibility crisis where AI-generated claims cannot be traced to rigorous evidence, wasting lab resources.

The Innovation

PromptGenix decouples reasoning (Bayesian engine) from explanation (LLM). While LLMs provide the narrative, our deterministic engine computes posterior confidence based on empirical evidence objects. This ensures every claim is grounded and reproducible—shifting AI from a "black-box generator" to a "transparent evidence synthesizer."

Phase I Specific Aims

Focus: Feasibility & Trust

  • Automate: standard evidence-extraction pipelines using selected public datasets (GEO, SRA)
  • Rank: implement a Bayesian decision engine to compute posterior confidence for hypotheses
  • Validate: demonstrate traceability, reproducibility across reruns, and usefulness for study decisions
  • Measure: time-to-hypothesis, rerun consistency, and pilot “useful” ratings
Boundary: Phase I evaluation uses public datasets only to avoid initial IP and data-use constraints.
Why it matters

Faster, more rigorous science—without requiring more expert bandwidth.

PromptGenix helps research teams move from “outputs” to “decisions” by producing ranked hypotheses with transparent uncertainty. This enables clearer experimental planning, more reproducible interpretation, and improved reviewer confidence in how conclusions were reached.

Phase II path: Phase I de-risks the core decision engine; Phase II scales to broader modalities, multi-site usage, and secure deployments.
