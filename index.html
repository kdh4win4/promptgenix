<section id="problem">

  <h2>The Problem</h2>

  <div class="grid-2">

    <div>

      <p>

        Modern immunology and genomics labs collect terabytes of RNA-seq and flow cytometry data,

        but analysis still depends on fragmented tools, manual scripts, and a handful of experts.

        Turnaround time is measured in weeks, not hours, and every project feels like a one-off.

      </p>

    </div>

    <div class="card">

      <h3>Today’s bottlenecks</h3>

      <ul>

        <li>Multiple disconnected tools (STAR, RSEM, FlowJo, COMPASS, GSEA)</li>

        <li>Custom scripts that are hard to reuse and harder to maintain</li>

        <li>Limited access to biostatistics & bioinformatics experts</li>

        <li>Scientific interpretation left to overworked PIs and analysts</li>

      </ul>

    </div>

  </div>

</section>


<section id="solution">

  <h2>The PromptGenix Platform</h2>

  <div class="grid-2">

    <div class="card">

      <h3>Automated pipelines</h3>

      <ul>

        <li>RNA-Seq: QC → alignment → quantification → DEG → GSEA/GSVA</li>

        <li>Flow cytometry: preprocessing, auto-gating, batch normalization</li>

        <li>COMPASS: automated polyfunctionality analysis for vaccines & trials</li>

        <li>scRNA-seq: Seurat / scanpy integration and cell-type reporting</li>

      </ul>

    </div>

    <div class="card">
